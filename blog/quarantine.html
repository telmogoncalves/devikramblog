Lets Make A Good Use Of "Quarantine"

Lets Make A Good Use Of "Quarantine". I sound crazy right, while people are dying, we are lockup in our countries, we are ordered to stay at home, we dont even know whether we will be alive tomorrow,and you are telling use to make a good use of this. All this are just talks, you are right ,we dont know if we will be alive tomorrow, but what if we are?.

If we are alive till the end of this dreadful disease, do you know we will regret not using our time wisely?, You dont?. If we use this #StayHomeOrder to learn coding, to learn new skills, do you know it is beneficial to us. You started learning programming 2 years ago, you have learnt just 2 languages because you had to go to school, go to work, read for exams e.t.c, it slows your learning, right now you are at home, in a locked up country, movement restricted. It is just a waste of time. Remember, "Time is going nowhere, but we are going somewhere". So, use this time to learn skills, make it the most memorable disease experience for your self, no one can make you happy than you. So are you in this with me?.
